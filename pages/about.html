about

I am a PhD candidate in the department of biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. Here's my CV and publication list.

For my dissertation, I'm developing statistical methods for detecting differential gene expression with RNA sequencing data. I'm advised by Jeff Leek and I collaborate closely with the Hopkins Genomics Working Group.

I spent the summer of 2013 writing code at Hacker School in New York City.
